Wholesale Accounts

We’re happy to provide wholesale pricing to customers purchasing our products to finish goods for sale or for resale. Provide an ST-5 (or other sales-tax exemption form) and we’ll update your account to tax-exempt. Distributor Accounts

Once your account surpasses $5,000 in total purchases, we’ll upgrade you to Distributor pricing. You’ll receive the 33.33% Bulk Discount Tier on every order (with ST-5 on file).

Contract Pricing

If you have an ST-5 on file, consistent monthly volume, and a solid working relationship with us, let’s talk. We often partner with volume customers to ensure long-term cooperation. Contract pricing is generally available for accounts exceeding $5,000/month. Typical transfer contract pricing can be as low as 50% off retail (e.g., $11.99 × 50% = $5.99).

FAQ

What exactly comes in the pack?▾
A mix of standard DTF plus specialty finishes (UV-DTF, Metallic, Reflective, Glow, Glitter) so you can test on real garments and hard goods.
What file format should I use when I order?▾
300dpi PNG with transparent background (true size). Convert fonts to shapes before export.
What press settings are typical?▾
Most users see great results with 285–305°F, 12–15s, medium pressure; peel per finish. Always test your blanks first.
Do I get a discount after trying?▾
Yes—there’s a 50% off code inside the pack for your first order.
