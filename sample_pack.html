Wholesale Accounts

We’re happy to provide wholesale pricing to customers purchasing our products to finish goods for sale or for resale. Provide an ST-5 (or other sales-tax exemption form) and we’ll update your account to tax-exempt. With an ST-5 on file you’ll be automatically upgraded to a Wholesale Account and receive the 20% Bulk Discount Tier—no matter your order size.

Distributor Accounts

Once your account surpasses $5,000 in total purchases, we’ll upgrade you to Distributor pricing. You’ll receive the 33.33% Bulk Discount Tier on every order (with ST-5 on file).

Contract Pricing

If you have an ST-5 on file, consistent monthly volume, and a solid working relationship with us, let’s talk. We often partner with volume customers to ensure long-term cooperation. Contract pricing is generally available for accounts exceeding $5,000/month. Typical transfer contract pricing can be as low as 50% off retail (e.g., $11.99 × 50% = $5.99).
